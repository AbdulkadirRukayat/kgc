It is a compilation of the life experiences of women who have proceeded from being naive adolescents to full-grown adults with families of their own. Those who have arrived at the end of the tunnel have the responsibility of lighting the path for others to come so that they would not miss their steps.

Our Da'wah Signpost Project In our continued effort to revive and propagate the Sunnah across Ebiraland, the United Muslim Sisters of Kogi Central (UMS-KGC) initiated a Dawah Signpost Project. These signposts are more than just displays; they are silent reminders strategically placed in public spaces to reconnect hearts with the words of Allah and the teachings of Islam.

UMS-KGC Beauty Lounge - A Vision for Halal Self-Care The UMS-KGC Beauty Lounge was born out of a deep desire to make self-care accessible, comfortable, and halal for Muslim women in our community. However, due to financial challenges and low turnout caused by the location, we had to pause operations. Though the doors are temporarily closed, the vision is not abandoned. We remain hopeful that with support and better positioning, we can revive this initiative.

UMS-KGC Ramadan Feeding Project Every Ramadan, the United Muslim Sisters of Kogi Central come together to ease the burden of fasting for the less privileged through our annual Ramadan Feeding Project. It is a heartfelt initiative aimed at providing food and support to needy families, widows, and vulnerable individuals during the blessed month.
